Our Academic Programme

At K. Garden Groove College Buddo, we provide a balanced curriculum that develops students academically, socially and creatively. Our dedicated teachers guide learners to achieve excellence and prepare them for future challenges.

🔬 Science Department

Providing practical learning in Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics through modern laboratories.

🌍 Humanities Department

Developing critical thinking through Geography, History, Religious Education and Social Sciences.

🌐 Languages Department

Improving communication skills through English and other language studies.

💻 ICT Department

Preparing students with computer skills, programming knowledge and digital literacy.

📊 Business Department

Building entrepreneurship and financial skills through business studies.
