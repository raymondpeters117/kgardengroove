Admissions at K. Garden Groove College Buddo

We welcome students who are ready to learn, grow and become responsible leaders. Our admission process is designed to help every learner join a supportive and excellent learning environment.

Admission Process

1️⃣ Submit Application

Complete the admission form and provide required documents.

2️⃣ Assessment

Students may undergo an interview or academic assessment.

3️⃣ Admission Offer

Successful applicants receive admission confirmation.

4️⃣ Registration

Complete registration and prepare to begin learning.

Admission Requirements

  • ✔ Previous school academic reports
  • ✔ Passport size photographs
  • ✔ Student identification documents
  • ✔ Parent/Guardian identification
  • ✔ Transfer documents for continuing students
  • ✔ Completed admission application form
